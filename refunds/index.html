Refunds Policy

PyCon Pune ticket bookings can be canceled for a full refund if done so up to 60 days before the commencement of the event. Requests for refunds will not be entertained after that.

For a cancellation and refund request, please send an email to pyconpune@pycon.org with details of your request. Once your request is received, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund along with details of the refund amount within 2 business days. If your refund is approved then it will be processed and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within 30 days.

Late or missing refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet:

  • First check your bank account again.
  • Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before our Fefund is officially posted.
  • Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.
  • If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at pyconpune@pycon.org

Pycon Pune 2017

Pune, India

February 16th - 19th

About

PyCon, the gathering for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. This is the first year of the PyCon Pune where the community will meet for two days of talks and working on upstream projects in two days of dev sprint.

Speakers

PyCon is proud to once again offer a stellar array of tutorials prior to PyCon's main conference days! These are multi-hour tutorial taught by experienced Pythonistas and instructors.

Conference

PyCon is the largest annual gathering for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language.

Sprints

Development sprints is the key part of PyCon, and a chance for the contributors to open-source projects together face-to-face for up to four days of intensive learning, development and camaraderie.

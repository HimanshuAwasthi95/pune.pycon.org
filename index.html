About PyCon Pune

PyCon, the gathering for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. This is the second year of the PyCon Pune where the community will meet for two days of talks and working on upstream projects in two days of dev sprint.

PyCon Pune is a community event and is volunteer-driven. People from the community come forward to volunteer and help make the conference better.
